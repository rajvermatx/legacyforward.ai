## Overview

CareerAlign is a static website. We do not collect, store, or process personal data directly.

## No Accounts or Tracking

We do not require user accounts, do not use cookies for tracking, and do not run analytics scripts.

## Third-Party Services

Our site loads resources from third-party services: Google Fonts (for typography), Google Colab (notebook links open in Google's service), GitHub (source code hosting). These services have their own privacy policies.

## Local Storage

We use browser localStorage solely to remember your theme preference (light/dark mode). This data never leaves your browser.

## External Links

Our site contains links to external websites and services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites.

## Changes

We may update this policy at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
