## The Problem

The AI landscape is chaotic. Vendor promises outpace reality, 85% of initiatives fail, and boards demand accountability for investments that ship but never move a metric. The LegacyForward.ai framework moves you from that chaos to clarity.

What goes wrong, in almost every case, is that someone makes a technology decision before they answer a business question. They choose a platform before they define the problem. They build a model before they understand what signal they are chasing. They deploy into production without a clear theory of how the existing environment will absorb the change.

The 15% that deliver value are not smarter or better-funded. They just do those things in the right order. This book applies the LegacyForward.ai framework to AI leadership, so you can fund, direct, and hold accountable the AI initiatives on your watch.

## The LegacyForward Framework

**Signal Capture** is the discipline of identifying what actually matters before you invest. Most organizations have more data than they can use and fewer clear signals than they think. Signal Capture asks: what decision will improve if we have this? What outcome changes? What does success look like in terms a finance team would recognize? Without that clarity upfront, AI projects drift from interesting to expensive to abandoned.

**Grounded Delivery** is how you manage the gap between what AI can do in a demo and what it does reliably in production. Language models hallucinate. Recommendation systems degrade. Computer vision fails on edge cases that were not in the training set. Grounded Delivery is the planning approach that accounts for the probabilistic nature of AI outputs, so you can still make commitments and hit them.

**Legacy Coexistence** addresses the architectural and organizational reality that most AI strategies ignore. Your data lives in systems built in 2009. Your workflows predate the word "LLM." Your team runs on processes that were designed before the tools you are now deploying on top of them. Legacy Coexistence is the framework for designing AI that works with what is there, not against it. The rip-and-replace approach fails at a rate that should embarrass anyone who still recommends it.

## How This Book Applies It

This book is for leaders who make the calls: budget calls, vendor calls, team calls, strategic calls. These are decisions made in an environment where everyone has an opinion and few people have accountability. The framework runs through every chapter. Signal Capture teaches you what to fund and what to decline. Grounded Delivery teaches you what to expect from your teams and your vendors. Legacy Coexistence teaches you what is realistic given the organization you lead today, not a greenfield one you wish you had.
